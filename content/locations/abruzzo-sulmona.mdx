Discover Sulmona, one of Italy's best-kept secrets for film and photography productions. This medieval jewel in Abruzzo offers extraordinary filming opportunities with its perfectly preserved historic center, distinctive ancient aqueduct, and stunning piazzas—all set against a backdrop of mountain landscapes.

## LOCATION HIGHLIGHTS

- Historic Roman aqueduct - Striking ancient arches cutting through the city center
- Medieval piazza with distinctive paving - Elegant public square with geometric stone patterns
- Renaissance architecture - Well-preserved historic buildings and civic structures
- Charming café culture - Authentic Italian outdoor dining settings
- Historic clock tower and church - Classical Italian architectural elements
- Mountain backdrop - Dramatic natural settings surrounding the urban environment

## IDEAL FOR PRODUCTIONS

Period Productions:

- Medieval and Renaissance period films
- Historical documentaries and drama series
- Period romance productions
- Cultural and historical content

Commercial Filming:

- Luxury Italian lifestyle campaigns
- Authentic Italian culinary photography
- Fashion shoots in distinctive historic settings
- Travel and tourism promotional content

## UNIQUE ADVANTAGES

- Unspoiled medieval character - Authentic Italian atmosphere without tourist crowds
- Versatile shooting environments - Historic architecture, public squares, and cafés
- Distinctive architectural elements - Roman aqueduct provides unique visual backdrop
- Authenticity factor - Real Italian daily life continues uninterrupted
- Limited filming traffic - Easy access to key locations without typical production challenges
- Visual continuity - Consistent architectural style throughout the historic center

## LOGISTICAL BENEFITS

- 2 hours from Rome
- Accessible via major highways
- Local accommodation options
- Supportive local authorities for filming permissions
- Minimal modern infrastructure disrupting historical views
- Year-round filming possibilities

Sulmona offers international productions the rare opportunity to capture authentic Italian medieval atmosphere in a virtually undiscovered location. The town's Roman aqueduct, elegant piazzas, and genuine Italian café culture provide diverse filming environments within easy walking distance—all without the location challenges typically found in more famous Italian destinations.
