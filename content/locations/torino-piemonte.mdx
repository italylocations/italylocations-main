Discover Turin (Torino), one of Italy's most sophisticated urban filming locations, offering wide boulevards, historic porticoes, and magnificent galleries rarely found elsewhere in Europe. This elegant northern Italian city presents production companies with exceptional architectural backdrops, classic European urban settings, and proximity to both the Alps and metropolitan Milan.

## EXCEPTIONAL LOCATION FEATURES

- Historic porticoed boulevards - Miles of covered walkways with elegant stone arches
- Stunning historic galleries - Spectacular glass-roofed shopping arcades with ornate details
- Majestic tramway streets - Distinctive cobblestone boulevards with classic tram lines
- Pristine urban environment - Exceptionally clean and well-maintained cityscapes
- Dramatic interior spaces - Marble floors, vaulted ceilings, and historical architectural details
- Alpine backdrop - Mountain views framing urban settings

## IDEAL FOR PRODUCTIONS

Film & Television:

- European period dramas requiring authentic 19th century urban settings
- Contemporary urban narratives requiring sophisticated backdrops
- Fashion and luxury brand storytelling
- Historical productions set in elegant European capitals

Commercial Applications:

- Luxury automotive campaigns along wide, elegant boulevards
- High-end fashion photography beneath historic porticoes
- Lifestyle and travel productions in refined urban settings
- Product photography utilizing distinctive architectural framing

## UNIQUE ADVANTAGES

- Architectural diversity - Belle Époque, Baroque, and modern elements within walking distance
- Exceptional light quality - Clear northern Italian light with alpine influences
- Visual sophistication - Elegant, understated atmosphere with minimal tourist interference
- Spacious urban layout - Wide streets and open plazas suitable for technical equipment
- Marble and glass features - Distinctive reflective surfaces and textures for visual interest
- Seasonal versatility - Beautiful settings in all seasons, from summer light to winter Alpine views

## LOGISTICAL BENEFITS

- Just 1.5 hours from Milan by high-speed train
- International airport with connections across Europe
- Close proximity to the Alps for combining urban/mountain shoots
- Excellent production infrastructure
- Supportive local film commission
- High-quality accommodations and services

Turin represents an extraordinary opportunity for productions seeking elegant European urban environments without the logistical challenges of more congested filming destinations. Its distinctive covered walkways, magnificent galleries, and clean, spacious boulevards offer versatile backdrops for diverse production needs.
