Discover Tuscania, an extraordinary medieval hamlet nestled in the Tuscia region that offers the authentic charm of Tuscany without the crowds. This perfectly preserved town provides filmmakers with atmospheric stone courtyards, ancient fountains, historic archways, and authentic Italian streetscapes rarely found elsewhere.

## LOCATION HIGHLIGHTS

- Authentic medieval architecture - Exceptionally preserved buildings with original features
- Atmospheric town squares - Intimate enclosed spaces with ancient stonework
- Historic church facades - Remarkable Romanesque churches of San Pietro and Santa Maria Maggiore
- Traditional stone archways - Delicate framing elements throughout the historic center
- Cobblestone streets - Original medieval pathways with distinctive patterns
- Textured stone walls - Beautifully weathered surfaces in warm Tuscan hues
- Panoramic viewpoints - Breathtaking vistas over the surrounding countryside
- Ancient Etruscan elements - Blending of medieval and Etruscan architectural details

## IDEAL FOR PRODUCTIONS

Period Productions:

- Medieval and Renaissance historical dramas
- Period costume films and series
- Classical documentaries
- Fantasy productions requiring authentic medieval settings

Modern Applications:

- Fashion photography contrasting contemporary style with ancient settings
- Luxury brand storytelling in authentic environments
- Food and lifestyle content in pristine Italian locations
- Commercial productions seeking unspoiled Italian backdrops

## UNIQUE ADVANTAGES

- Preserved medieval center - Exceptionally intact historic core
- Absence of modern intrusions - Few contemporary elements disrupting historic views
- Minimal tourist presence - Authentic historical atmosphere without crowds
- Textural richness - Stone, wood, and plaster surfaces with centuries of patina
- Intimate scale - Walkable filming environments with diverse settings
- Contrast of ancient and contemporary - Modern boutiques and cafés in medieval settings

## LOGISTICAL BENEFITS

- Just 100 minutes from Rome
- Easy access via major highways
- Good accommodation options
- Supportive local film office
- Flexible filming permits
- Low-cost options available
- Year-round filming possibility

Tuscania offers international productions a rare opportunity to capture authentic medieval Italian atmosphere without the location challenges typically found in more tourist-heavy destinations. The town's architectural consistency, unspoiled historical features, and close proximity to Rome make it an ideal location for productions seeking genuine Italian medieval settings.
