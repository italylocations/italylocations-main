Discover Viterbo, an extraordinary medieval filming location just 90 minutes from Rome that offers production companies authentic historic settings virtually untouched by modern intervention. This perfectly preserved medieval city provides filmmakers with atmospheric stone courtyards, ancient fountains, historic archways, and authentic Italian streetscapes rarely found elsewhere.

## LOCATION HIGHLIGHTS

- Medieval stone architecture - Exceptionally preserved buildings with original features
- Atmospheric courtyards - Intimate enclosed spaces with arched colonnades
- Historic water features - Ancient fountains with distinctive lion spouts
- Traditional stone archways - Dramatic framing elements throughout the city
- Authentic wooden doors - Original medieval entrances with distinctive hardware
- Textured stone walls - Beautifully weathered surfaces in warm Italian hues
- Cobblestone streets - Historic paving patterns providing authentic atmosphere
- Blend of rustic and refined - Medieval architecture alongside modern boutique settings

## IDEAL FOR PRODUCTIONS

Period Productions:

- Medieval and Renaissance historical dramas
- Period romance films and series
- Historical documentaries
- Fantasy productions requiring authentic medieval settings

Modern Applications:

- Fashion photography contrasting contemporary style with ancient settings
- Luxury brand storytelling in authentic environments
- Travel and lifestyle content in distinctive Italian locations
- Culinary productions highlighting traditional Italian cuisine

## UNIQUE ADVANTAGES

- Preserved medieval quarter - Exceptionally intact historic center
- Diverse architectural elements - Towers, arches, courtyards, and fountains
- Minimal modern intrusions - Authentic historical atmosphere
- Textural richness - Stone, wood, and plaster surfaces with centuries of patina
- Intimate scale - Walkable filming environments with diverse settings
- Contrast of ancient and contemporary - Modern boutiques and cafés in medieval settings

## LOGISTICAL BENEFITS

- Just 90 minutes from Rome
- Easy access via major highways
- Local accommodation options
- Supportive local film office
- Minimal tourist crowds
- Year-round filming possibility

Viterbo offers international productions a rare opportunity to capture authentic medieval Italian atmosphere without the location challenges typically found in more famous destinations. The city's exceptional preservation, diverse architectural features, and close proximity to Rome make it an ideal location for productions seeking genuine historical settings with practical production advantages.
